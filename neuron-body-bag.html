PRODUCT DESCRIPTION

The Neuron Body Bag treats the body as a moving network. The strap becomes an edge across the torso, while the pockets, rings, seams, and openings behave like nodes for access and adjustment.

The piece is designed to sit between accessory and garment: structured enough to carry objects, soft enough to collapse into the body, and detailed enough to remain visible as part of the silhouette.
